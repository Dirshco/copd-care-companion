🌬️ Proper Inhaler Technique

For MDI (Metered Dose Inhaler):
  1. Remove cap and shake inhaler well (5 seconds)
  2. Breathe out fully, away from inhaler
  3. Place mouthpiece between teeth, seal lips tightly
  4. Start breathing in SLOWLY, then press canister
  5. Continue inhaling deeply (3-5 seconds)
  6. Hold breath for 10 seconds, then exhale slowly
  7. Wait 1 minute between puffs if taking multiple
For Dry Powder Inhaler (HandiHaler):
  1. Open blister pack, place capsule in chamber
  2. Pierce capsule by pressing buttons firmly
  3. Breathe out fully (away from device)
  4. Seal lips around mouthpiece, inhale DEEPLY and STEADILY
  5. Hold breath 10 seconds, exhale slowly
  6. Check capsule is empty; repeat if needed

⚠️ Common Mistakes: Not shaking MDI, inhaling too fast, not holding breath, not using spacer if prescribed.
